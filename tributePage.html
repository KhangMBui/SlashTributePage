Slash

Saul Hudson - The man who speaks with his guitar

Guns N' Roses-including Axl Rose, Slash, Duff McKagan, Izzy Stradlin, and Steven Adler-performing in the 80s

Career

1981-1985: Slash joined his first band, Tidus Sloan, in 1981. In 1983, he formed Road Crew (named after a Motorhead song) with Steven Adler. He and Adler then met Duff Mckagan and together they joined Hollywood Rose

1985-1996: "Guns N' Roses", the most dangerous band in the world, is formed by Slash, Axl, Izzy, Duff, and Steven. Their first album Appetite for Destruction is the bestselling debut album of all time in the U.S. Followed by Use your Illusion I & II, it didn't take long for Guns N' Roses to rule the world.

1994-2002: "Slash's Snakepit" is Slash's solo project after his departure from Guns N' Roses. These are the work he wanted to put on GnR's next record but couldn't due to Axl's ego.

2002-2008: "Velvet Revolver". In 2002, Slash reunited with Duff and Matt and decided to form a Velvet Revolver with their vocalist Dave Kushner.

2009-present: "Slash featuring Myles Kennedy and The Conspirators". In September 2008, Slash began his solo project with Myles Kennedy and The Conspirators and released notable albums.

2016-present: "Return to Guns N' Roses". YES, what everyone have been waiting for for 20 years. Axl, Duff, and Slash reunites Guns N' Roses and rule the world once again.
